# Space Monkey Films (2023)
<Thick>[spacemonkeyfilms.us](https://spacemonkeyfilms.us/)</Thick>

## About

> Custom videography WordPress theme with a custom post types, taxonomies, and galleries.

Space Monkey Films is a video production company based in San Francisco, CA. They needed a website to showcase their work and provide a way for potential clients to contact them. I built a custom WordPress theme with a custom post type for their videos, a custom taxonomy for their services, and a custom gallery post type for their galleries. The theme also includes a custom contact form and a custom admin page for managing the galleries.
