## Ford E-Series Window & Door-Lock Switch Covers (Cat-Proof)

Snap-on guards for the power window and door-lock switches on Ford E-Series vans and RV chassis (E-150 / E-250 / E-350 / E-450 Econoline, including 1999–2007 Super Duty motorhome frames such as the Fleetwood Jamboree). They keep curious pets — and stray elbows — from bumping the switches while you drive. Designed to be printed in PLA, PETG, or TPU. Four layouts are included to match different switch clusters.
